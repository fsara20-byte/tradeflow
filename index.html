Tokenised ownership

The token records fractional economic ownership of a legally assigned receivable.

The platform tokenises the economic interest in the receivable’s cash flow. The invoice is verified, assigned to an SPV, funded by investors, then repaid when the buyer pays the SPV collection account.

1

Legal assignment

Receivable transferred into a bankruptcy-remote SPV with notice and buyer confirmation.

2

Token registry

Investor allocations are recorded as tokenised ownership units tied to the underlying receivable.

3

Cash-flow distribution

At maturity, buyer repayment is distributed to token holders based on ownership percentage.
