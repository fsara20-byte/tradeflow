Verified trade receivables, structured for qualified capital.

TradeFlow is a marketplace concept that converts confirmed invoice receivables into professionally structured investment opportunities, giving suppliers earlier liquidity and investors access to short-duration trade credit.

Concept website for demonstration and fundraising discussion. Licensing, product terms and investor eligibility would require formal regulatory approval.

Approach

A serious alternative to generic invoice financing.

The platform is designed around receivable verification, legal assignment, investor suitability and controlled risk allocation. It is not a crypto app and not a simple invoice scoring tool.

For suppliers

Earlier liquidity

SMEs can submit confirmed receivables and receive an advance after document checks, buyer confirmation and pricing approval.

For investors

Short-duration credit access

Eligible investors can access receivable-backed opportunities by buyer quality, duration and target return profile.

For banks

Referral and distribution channel

Banks can refer small-ticket trade finance opportunities that may not fit their balance sheet economics.

Platform

Built like a market infrastructure layer.

Each receivable is treated as a financial asset with documentation, risk controls, investor disclosures and repayment tracking.

Risk architecture

Investor confidence depends on controls, not design language.

Receivable fraud

Buyer confirmation, duplicate detection and assignment checks before any receivable is listed.

Buyer default

Exposure limits, buyer scoring, reserves and optional insurance for selected pools.

Platform insolvency

SPV separation, ring-fenced accounts and backup servicing plan.

Regulatory risk

RegLab-first path, legal opinions and product classification before launch.
